### `bluetime.paymentGateway.performAuthorizeOperation`<a id="bluetimepaymentgatewayperformauthorizeoperation"></a>

This method allows you to perform an "authorize" operation (also called "preauthorization" or "authorization") through your payment gateway. When you perform the "authorize" operation, the bank that issued the credit card will place an authorization of the total amount of the funds.
